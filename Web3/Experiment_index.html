What is Minecraft ?

Minecraft is a game made up of blocks, creatures, and community. You can survive the night or build a work of art – the choice is all yours. But if the thought of exploring a vast new world all on your own feels overwhelming, then fear not! Let’s explore what Minecraft is all about!

How to play Minecraft

There are many types of player in minecraft but i will only put 5 of them

  1. Break Wood to get Log.
  2. Make a Crafting Table and use the Crafting Table to make a Wooden Pickaxe & a Furnace.
  3. Get stone and Upgrade your Wooden Pickaxe into Stone Pickaxe.
  4. Get Raw Iron and Coal and smelt the Raw Iron with Coal in the Furnace.
  5. Make a full set of Iron armor, an Iron Pickaxe and an Iron Axe.
  6. If you don't have enough materials go back and find more Iron.
  7. Now try to find as many diamond you can and make a diamond armor & diamond gear.
  8. After that try to go to the nether and find a nether fotress and kill blazes until you have 6 blaze rod.
  9. Then Try to find a piglin and trade gold with them until you get 12 enderpearls.
  10. Craft 12 eyes of ender and try to follow where the eyes of ender is going.
  11. After you found the stronghold fill the portal and fight the ender dragon in the end.
  12. And there you go, you beat the game.
